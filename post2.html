Co-op Internship

President's Choice Financial Bank IT Center-of-Excellence Application Team

Between my third and final year of college, I completed a 16-month internship as part of the Co-op Program from May 2018 to August 2019.

I worked for the child company President's Choice Financial supporting the Bank operations in the Application Team, parent company is Canada's #1 food and retail provider Loblaw.

I participated in many daily IT operations and followed an Application Release Schedule working alongside other IT analysts, project managers, and third-party consultants.

In December 2018, approximately 6 months into my internship I received an award: Certificate of Recognition for Oustanding Vulnerability Management by my Senior Director Janet Lin.

I was constantly working with technical documentation, ticket management systems, and enterprise platforms to maintain enterprise-wide cyber-security compliance standards.

I arranged meetings and touchpoints where appropriate to allow for effective communication and consistently uploaded on SharePoint for the entire team to be on same page.

Technologies and Concepts Learned:

  • - Cyber-security - vulnerability management
  • - ServiceNow - SaaS
  • - Application support
