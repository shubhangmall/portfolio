Product Development Project

ModFrames: Modular Customizable Detachable Glass Frames

In my final year of college, I took a Product Development Engineering course which involved teams of 4 creating and designing a new product.

The product that my team and I came up with solves a problem that faces all eye-wearing individuals.

We designed an inexpensive frame that can be detached with switchable parts so that it fits all shapes and sizes.

There was a specific product engineering design process that was followed including creating a business plan and final presentation.

Due to COVID-19, the presentation was moved to an online video that can be seen below.

This project was successful as we received an A while keeping in mind the project requirements.

Technologies and Concepts Learned:
  • - Mechanical design project process
  • - Generating multiple design alternatives
  • - User insight analysis
  • - Financial justification process
  • - CAD
  • - 3D printer - laser cutting
  • - Design solutions
  • - Presentations & reports
